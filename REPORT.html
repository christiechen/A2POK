Question 1

Daily Schedule of a GasTech Employee

A very general layout of a single workday for a GasTech employee.

  • 7:30-7:50 AM — Daily coffee at the place of choice We can see that employees are clustured around coffee shops on the way to/around the GasTech building: Jack's Magical Beans, Hallowed Grounds, Cup o' Joe, Brewe've Been Served, and Katerina's Cafe.
  • 8-8:30 AM — Employees seem to start working in this time period. Security starts to drive to GasTech around 8, and most Engineers join by 8:20.
  • 12-1:30PM — Lunch break starts. Following a good 2-3 hours of mostly inactivity after people arrive at work, there is a flurry of activity from the company cars in all departments starting at 12. Employees mostly buy lunch or run errands; some go to the grocery store or visit other nearby areas on the way back to work.
  • 1:30-2PM — Lunch break ends. Company cars in all departments start to drive back towards GasTech at 1:30. By hovering over the dots and looking at tooltips in the visualization, you can track the movement of the dots.
  • Evening — In the evening, employees may host gatherings at bars or generally meet up. They also run personal errands, such as grocery shopping, etc...
